About Me

People look at me and see a smart, green-eyed, redheaded who has a calm personality. But in reality, I am so much more. I am a born Canadian, who is proud of her culture, and at the same time, is proud of the fact that my background is part Indian and part British. I always like to explore these cultures; whether it is reading Indian mythology with my sister, eating spicy Indian food with my hands or trying different kinds of British sweets(candies). Another thing that most people fail to see at a first glance, is how much I love to dance. The second I hear music, I start to groove whether it is tapping my foot to the beat or casually dancing in a grocery store. Also, I am the best roommate. While everyone is screaming due to a spider in the room, I am the one who will gently take it outside. And on top of all of that, how many girls do you know who want their own TARDIS?
